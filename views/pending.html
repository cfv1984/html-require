Missing stuff

  • Request caching. Each piece gets loaded over the wire every single time
  • Lifecycle events: There's no real notion of a lifecycle here and that could use some love
  • Transformations? Going on a limb here but being able to dynamically parse SASS or HAML sounds awesome
